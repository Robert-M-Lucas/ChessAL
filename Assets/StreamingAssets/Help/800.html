Viking Chess

Despite its name, Viking Chess is completely different from normal chess

The Viking Chess board

Moving

All pieces can move like rooks in chess, that is, move in any direction any amount of places (not diagonally). No piece other than the king can enter the corner squares, which ends the game, or the centre square.

King can move (top-left) to corner to win

Taking pieces

To take a piece, you must surround it on two opposite sides with your pieces. A piece can be moved between two enemy pieces without being taken. The king cannot be used to take pieces. If a piece is by the corner square, it can be taken against that square with a single piece.

Black can move to take white

White can take black against corner (bottom-left) square

To take the king it must be surrounded on four sides or, if against a wall, three. This ends the game.

Black can move to encircle king against wall and win
